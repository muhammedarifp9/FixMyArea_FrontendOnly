How FixMyArea Works

Simple steps to a cleaner and better city.

1. Report

Snap a photo and pin the location of any civic issue. It's fast, easy, and impactful.

2. Track

Monitor progress in real-time. Upvote critical issues to ensure they get the attention they deserve.

3. Resolve

Verify the fix once completed. We close the loop together with the local authorities.
